All About Us

TAIFUU Yosakoi Dance Crew

TAIFUU Yosakoi dance crew belongs to the Japanese club of Foreign Trade University. The dance crew gathers people who are passionate about dancing and love Japanese culture. We always want to spread the joyful spirit and positive energy of Yosakoi dance to everyone.

FJC_Foreign Trade Univesiry Japanese Club

FJC, the abbreviation of FTU Japanese Club, is the Japanese Club of Foreign Trade University HCM City. FJC will bring you the most interesting knowledge about the culture and language of Japan, the country.
